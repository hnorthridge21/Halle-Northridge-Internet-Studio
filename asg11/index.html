The Dodgeball Dream Team

Meet *The Thunderbolts* — a team of fearless, fast, and slightly chaotic legends ready to dominate the dodgeball court. From strength to strategy, each member brings something unique to the game.

1. Lara Croft

Agile, precise, and fiercely competitive. Lara’s survival instincts and quick reflexes make her an unstoppable dodgeball warrior.

2. Spider-Man

With his agility, web-slinging reflexes, and “spidey sense,” no one’s dodging or catching like this guy.

3. Wonder Woman

Armed with power and precision, Wonder Woman can throw with godlike strength and defend with unbeatable skill.

4. Sonic the Hedgehog

Speed wins games. Sonic can dodge, spin, and score before anyone even knows what happened.

5. Deadpool

For comic relief and unpredictable plays. He’s fearless, fast, and totally unbothered by getting hit (literally).
