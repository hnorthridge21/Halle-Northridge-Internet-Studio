Favorite Movies Gallery

I like each of these movies for their own unique reasons. However, I have them kind of split into categories. The first 4 are emotional, "real" movies, all kind of focusing on relationships. I love the Harold and Kumar series both because it is hilarious, and because my friends and I used to watch it together in highschool. The next 4 are all Studio Ghibli movies which I love for their deep story tellng and beautiful art. Lastly, Ratatoullie and Tarzan are two of my childhood favorites.

Babyteeth

Babyteeth is a 2019 Australian coming-of-age film following a young girl who falls in love just before a cancer recurrence.
