CatWoman

Hero or Villan?

Character Development

Catwoman was first debuted in spring of 1940 as "The Cat". Although she was first depicted as a supervillan and Batmans enemy, she has also been depicted as his love interest. She has become a sort of "anti-heroine" with a love-hate relationship with Batman. She is shown to follow a utilitarian moral philosophy, a sort of "robin hood" of the Batman universe. She was initially created with the idea of adding "sex-appeal" for the male gaze to the comics, but at the same time as a way to appeal more to female readers. They ended up creating a "friendly foe who committed crimes but was also a romantic interest in Batman's rather sterile life." She was meant to play a sort of cat and mouse chase/game with Batman who wanted to reform her. However, she was different from the other Batman universe villans in that she was never a killer or down right "evil".
