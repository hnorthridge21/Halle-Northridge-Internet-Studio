Welcome to Aoshima, the Cat Island

Aoshima Island or Cat Island is an island in Japan, famous for its massive population of cats which outnumbers its human population by almost 36:1.

Highlights

  • Over 100 cats live on the island.
  • Only accessible by ferry from Nagahama Port.
  • Peaceful island with very few human residents.
