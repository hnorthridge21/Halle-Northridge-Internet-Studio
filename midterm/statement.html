Design Statement

I chose Aoshima, also known as Cat Island, because it is unique, peaceful, and visually fun. The islandâ€™s cute cats inspired a calm, soft color palette and simple layout that reflects a beginner-friendly design.

The colors, fonts, and rounded buttons were selected to create a warm and welcoming feeling. The navigation menu is a Flexbox layout for easy positioning and consistent appearance across all pages.

For animation, I added a small hover effect to the navigation links. When you hover over a link, it grows slightly and changes color, making the site interactive without being complicated.
